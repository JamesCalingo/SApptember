Can you score?

With hockey and basketball seasons just around the corner, I figured I'd do this today.

Try to score a goal/bucket!
In order to score, you need to move the ball around to break down the defense (or defence). If you pass too many or too few times, your shot will miss/be blocked by the defenders.

Use the pass button to make passes, and once you think you have the right number to score, click the shoot button. You won't need any more than ten passes to score, so be careful not to get super pass happy.
