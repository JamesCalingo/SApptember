Being a fan of the Super Smash Bros. competitive scene, you decide to take one of its most famous mottos - "No Johns!" - to heart and kill John.

Kate surprises you by referencing this phrase while announcing John's death. Your affection for her grows after this moment...

...but then you remember that her fiancee had to leave earlier in the night because he has to be somewhere early tomorrow.

The results of the game don't matter anymore: all you can think about is Kate making a reference you thought no one else knew, how she would've learned it given that her fiancee is, as far as you know, a casual Smash player at best, and how you now have an unrequited love.
